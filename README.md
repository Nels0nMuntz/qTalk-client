# qTalk

qTalk is an application where users can create communities based on their interests, hobbies, or passions. In this vibrant online space, users can submit posts, share links, and engage in discussions within these communities. Each community, known as a subtalk, is dedicated to a specific topic, allowing like-minded people to connect and share their thoughts. Users can upvote or downvote posts and comments, determining their visibility and popularity within the community.
